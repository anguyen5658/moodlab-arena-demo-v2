Zone Mechanics

Core systems that power the Fortune Zone experience.

🎰

Daily Jackpot

Growing counter starting at ~47,382 coins. Increases over time, resets when won. Drives daily engagement and return visits.

✨

Lucky Hour

Random 2x multiplier on all wins during a surprise hour window. Unpredictable timing creates excitement and urgency.

📋

Hub Tabs

Two main navigation tabs: Sportsbook (WC predictions) and Fortune Games (12 casino-style games).
