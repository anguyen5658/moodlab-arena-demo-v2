Section 5

💬 Chat System

🌐 Global Chat

Available on hub screens. Open to all players in the lobby. General banter and community interaction.

🎮 In-Game Chat

Side-based messaging. Your side vs AI side. Chat only visible to fans on the same side.

🏁 Post-Game Chat

10-minute merged chat after game ends. Both sides can talk. Recap and reactions.

🤖 Bot Messages

7 bots with 10 message templates each. Auto-populate chat for lively atmosphere.

💥 Puff Reactions

Live reactions during games. Quick emoji blasts triggered by in-game puff events.
