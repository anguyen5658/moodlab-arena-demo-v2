3 FK2: Final Kick 2 — Double Puff

⚽🔥 FK2 Mechanics FK2

The Twist: No zone tapping! Two sequential puffs control X aim then Y aim. Zone is derived from combined X+Y values.

💨 Puff 1 — X position (left/right) 💨 Puff 2 — Y position (low/high) 👆 Tap — Save phase only

Double Puff Aim System

4 FK3: Final Kick 3D — Three.js

⚽🌐 FK3 Mechanics FK3

The Innovation: Full 3D behind-the-ball camera view using Three.js 0.160.0. Same double-puff as FK2 but rendered in 3D perspective.

💨 Puff 1 — X in 3D space 💨 Puff 2 — Y trajectory height 🚲 Touch/Tilt — Glove control (save)
