Dual Currency System
Two currencies, two purposes, one economy

🪙 Mood Coins

SPENDABLE CURRENCY
  • Earned from games, daily check-ins, challenges
  • Spent in the Shop on avatars, effects, themes
  • Can be risked in Fortune zone (Oracle games)
  • Multiplied by tier + device bonuses
  • CAN be lost (wagering in Slots, Blackjack, etc.)

⭐ XP (Experience)

PROGRESSION CURRENCY
  • Earned from games (+20 win / +10 loss), dailies, challenges
  • Drives tier progression: Bronze -> Legend
  • Accumulates forever, never decreases
  • Triggers tier-up events with confetti + notifications
  • NEVER lost -- only goes up

In-Game Integration
Loyalty is visible everywhere during active gameplay
🏅

Multiplier Badge

A persistent HUD badge (top-right corner during gameplay) shows your current tier multiplier icon and value. If BLE is connected, a second "1.5x" device badge stacks below it. Always visible so players know their bonus.

🎁

Floating Rewards

After each game result, a large animated "+N coins" and "+N XP" floats up from center screen with a gold glow and fades out over 2 seconds. The reward amount shown is already multiplied by the total multiplier.

🔥

Win Streak Bonuses

Consecutive wins trigger escalating bonuses: 3-streak notification, 5-streak +50 bonus coins, 10-streak +200 bonus coins with crowd sound effect. Streaks reset on loss with a notification if streak was 3+.

📈

Reward Breakdown

The recordGameResult function calculates: base coins times total multiplier, plus base XP + bonus (15 for win, 5 for loss). Tier-up detection runs after every XP award, triggering confetti and level-up sound.
